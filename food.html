Cuisines and Eateries

Nice is home to some of the finest and most reputable food in Europe. No visit to the French Riviera is complete without sampling some of the local Nice specialties.

In Nice, there is a mix of unique flavors only available in this region – traditional Provençal and Niçoise foods with a heavy Italian influence. Being on the Mediterranean, seafood and fresh ingredients play an integral role in the tasty regional specialties.

The cuisine of Nice is especially close to those of Provence but also Liguria and Piedmont and uses local ingredients (olive oil, anchovies, fruit and vegetables) but also those from more remote regions, in particular from Northern Europe, because ships which came to pick up olive oil arrived full of food products, such as dried haddock.

Nice has a few local dishes. There is a local tart made with onions and anchovies (or anchovy paste), named "Pissaladière". Socca is a type of pancake made from chickpea flour. Farcis niçois is a dish made from vegetables stuffed with a mixture of breadcrumbs, meat (generally sausage and ground beef), and herbs; and salade niçoise is a tomato salad with green peppers of the "Corne" variety, baked eggs, tuna or anchovies, and olives.

Local meat comes from neighbouring valleys, such as the sheep of Sisteron. Local fish, such as mullets, bream, sea urchins, and anchovies (alevins) are used to a great extent, so much so that it has given birth to a proverb: "fish are born in the sea and die in oil".

Highest Rated Nice Restaurants

Jan

It's a small restaurant with distinctive decor with classic and modern elements, great blue colour on the wall, this has for effect a very relaxing atmosphere. Service excellent, very attentive and welcoming.

Les Sens

Les Sans is home to an excellent team truly deserving of their Michelin. With first class attention to all details and fabulous food in their intimate dining room. Be sure to try the three courses with foie gras, cod fish and dessert with an apple tema.

Le Millesime

Visiting Nice and missing this restaurant should be considered a sin. Start with a nice French white wine and enjoy a 5-course meal where the dishes are original and very tasty.

Le Sejour Cafe

A tiny restaurant off the tourist piste with excellent food, great ambiance of the modern warm family touch, and professional service. Arrive in this little elegant oasis where each dish is full of taste. Try the green apple soup.

La Roustide

Tucked away in the suburbs you know you're onto something special with La Roustide. The setting is wonderful as the decor is tasteful; From wine to appetizer to main course to dessert! Every bite will make your trip to Nice a memorable experience.

MELODYNICE

Melodynice is not in the heart of Nice center but it's worth of visiting! Be prepared to be impressed with the great tasting high quality dishes and fantastic service.

Bistrot Gourmand

This is a superb restaurant with high quality food in lovely room, great service, very professional staff. Excellent for lunch and dinner. The location is very close to city centre so grabbing a table when your in the area!

Le Boudoir

It's a pleasure going to this restaurant where the food, service & ambiance are all great, resulting in a memorable experience. The duck is particularly sensational paired with a Rose Wine.

Vino & Cucina

Homeliness and excellent quality products come together for an unforgettable meal. Amazing food, service and wine! This place is off the beaten track but well worth the visit. Highly recommend the veal!
