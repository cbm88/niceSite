Hotspots in Nice

Love the beach? Love art? Love adventure? Nice is the perfect blend of all three.

When you touch down in Nice, the warm waters of the Mediterranean should be at the top of your itinerary. Situated on the Cote d'Azur, the beachfront draws hundreds of thousands to Nice. Your day at the beach can be perfectly accompanied by a long stroll on the famed boardwalk, the Promenade des Anglais.

Beyond the beach, Nice offers visitors a vibrant art scene steeped in history. If you enjoy modernism and surrealism, you will want to visit Marc Chagall's artwork at the Musee Marc Chagall and Henry Matisse's artwork at the Musee Matisse.

Nice is known for its open-air public squares, which are great for hanging out, taking pictures, shopping, cooling off by the water fountains, and viewing Greco-Roman influence on French architecture. The three most famous squares are:

  • Place Massena
  • Place Garibaldi
  • Place du Palais

Nice's cathedrals elegantly mix charm and artistic detail. You will want to see:

  • Notre Dame de Nice
  • Cathedrale de Nice
  • St. Nicholas Orthodox Cathedral

Just off the beaten path, you can venture to the Colline du Chateau. It features a stunning waterfall and a prewar fort that accepts visitors during the day.

So what are you waiting for? Nice's warm embrace awaits.
